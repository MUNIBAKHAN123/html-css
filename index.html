NEOM is a mega-project and a cross-border business and industrial region (special economic zone) being created in the Northwest of Saudi Arabia [1] [i]. [AG1] The project’s vision is to establish a “land of the future” fostering a thriving global community of pioneers [2], and aspires to promote economy, sustainability, and a new model for urban living [3]. NEOM is a central part of Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 plan to diversify its economy and reduce its dependence on oil and represents a bold endeavor to reshape Saudi Arabia's future, aligning with global trends in innovation, sustainability, and economic development [4].

Set in the northwestern part of the country along the Red Sea, NEOM covers a land area of around 26,500 square kilometers, and is comprised of distinct regions [5]. The five announced regions as of 2023 are:

  • THE LINE, a revolutionary linear city powered by renewable energy sources;
  • Oxagon, a center for advanced and clean industries
  • Trojena, a mountain destination;
  • Oxagon,Sindalah, a luxurious island resort;
  • Leyja, a luxury eco-tourism center. [6]

Neom will also include an airport as well as 6.5 km² of agricultural fields[7].NEOM’s economic, industrial, and touristic activities strive to attract investments and boost tourism in Saudi Arabia, which in turn seeks to create jobs directly in NEOM and indirectly across commercial and construction industries [8]. In this sense, Neom will be a destination for investors, entrepreneurs, and visitors, including tourists and local residents alike [9]. Notably, 95% of NEOM will become a natural reserve [10].

he project was launched in 2017 by Saudi Crown Prince Mohammed Bin Salman, and is primarily funded by the Public Investment Fund of Saudi Arabia (PIF) [11] [ii]. With a commitment of USD 500 billion, supplemented by contributions from local and international investors, it represents one of the largest investment projects globally [12]. In 2019, Saudi Arabia has announced setting up a closed joint-stock company named NEOM, wholly owned subsidiary of the PIF [13] . A few International studios have officially been announced as consultants, including multinational consulting studio AECOM and American engineering company Bechtel [14] [iii].

NEOM is progressing through a series of phases. Early phases focus on infrastructure development. OXAGON will welcome its first residents by 2024, while TROJENA is projected to become a vibrant place to live, work, and vacation in 2026 [15]. Furthermore, the first modules of THE LINE, an innovative linear city concept, are scheduled for activation in the same year (re mention the year) [16]. By 2030, NEOM anticipates accommodating approximately one million residents, with a projected population of nine million by 2045 [17]. The project is planned to become carbon-neutral by 2030 [18].
